The Maryland Institute College of Art’s (MICA) Decker Library will welcome Cristina Fontánez Rodríguez as its 2018–19 National Digital Stewardship Residency (NDSR) Art resident. Fontánez Rodríguez will begin her year-long digital stewardship residency with Decker Library’s Digital Initiatives Unit on July 16.
